Easy Lobster Ravioli Recipe

The lobster is simply enriched with mascarpone, aromatics, and fresh herbs, so the sweet lobster meat shines through in the finished ravioli. If you want to jazz it up, add some cayenne pepper, Parmesan, or saffron.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 (2 pound) boneless beef chuch roast, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
